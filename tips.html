How do I determine my correct foundation shade?

Determining your perfect foundation shade involves identifying your undertone (warm, cool, or neutral) and shade depth (light, medium, or dark). Try swatching shades on your jawline in natural light, considering coverage preference and skin concerns like redness or discoloration. Utilize shade-matching services offered by beauty retailers for personalized guidance in finding the ideal match for your complexion.

How should I store my cosmetics to prolong their shelf life?

Lto prolong the shelf life of your cosmetics, store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture.
